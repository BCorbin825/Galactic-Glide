Narrative
As a skilled spacecraft pilot, you must defend humanity's colonies from the alien Zaxons, battling through waves of enemy ships. Progress through the game's never ending level to face increasingly difficult challenges, reach new high scores, and save the galaxy from tyranny.  
Move
Use the directional keys or WASD to move
Fire
Hold or press space bar
Power-ups
Upgrade your spacecraft's weapons
Objective
Earn the highest score possible by shooting down as many enemy ships as you can while avoiding obstacles and enemy fire.
Difficulty
Normal and Hard. Normal mode has unlimited ammo and standard obstacles/enemies. Hard mode has more enemies/obstacles and limited ammo which restores over time or by hitting an enemy/obstacle.
Leaderboard
Highscore tracking so you push yourself to the limits. Can you climb to the top and become the ultimate Galactic Glide champion?
